About Us

VibeCircle is a social networking platform designed to connect people across Africa. Our mission is to bring together individuals, communities, and businesses, fostering a sense of unity and collaboration throughout the continent.

Features

  • Connect with friends and family
  • Share updates, photos, and videos
  • Join and create groups for your interests
  • Discover events happening around you
  • Support local businesses and initiatives
  • Chat with friends
  • Like and comment on posts
  • Track friends' locations (with permission)
  • Flirting feature
  • Post videos
  • Reject friend requests
  • Block users
  • Request a date with a friend
